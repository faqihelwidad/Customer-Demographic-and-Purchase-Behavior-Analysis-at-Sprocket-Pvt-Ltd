# Project Background

Sprocket Pvt Ltd is a bicycle and accessories retail company with a diverse customer base. To support a more targeted marketing strategy, an understanding of customer demographics and purchasing behavior is required.

This project analyzes customer demographic and transaction data over the past three years to identify purchasing patterns, key customer segments, and sales contributors. The results are presented through a dashboard to support data-driven marketing and business decision-making.

# Data Structure & Initial Checks

# Executive Summary

The analysis shows that customers aged 31–45 years dominate both customer distribution and sales volume. Female customers represent the largest customer group and contribute the most to total sales. Additionally, the Mass Customer wealth segment has the highest number of customers and generates the highest total sales.

These findings highlight key demographic segments that should be prioritized in marketing and retention strategies.

#Insights Deep Dive

### Customer Demographics

- The largest customer age group is 31–45 years.
- Most customers are female.
- Mass Customers make up the largest wealth segment.

### Sales Contribution

- The 31–45 years age group generates the highest number of sales.
- Female customers are the largest contributors to total sales.
- The Mass Customer segment records the highest total sales value.

# Recommendations

- Create engaging marketing campaigns highlighting the benefits of cycling to attract customers from underrepresented age groups.
- Develop a loyalty points program for Mass Customers whose purchases meet or exceed average sales to maintain segment stability.
- Offer discount promotions with minimum purchase requirements for High Net Worth and Affluent Customer segments to increase their transaction value.

# Assumptions and Caveats

The analysis is based solely on historical customer and transaction data.
